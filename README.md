# Part 1: Water
Immersion or cleaning in water marks purification, such as the *mikveh* in Judaism, or to mark a spritual initiation or dedication, such as the baptism in Christianity, are core parts of many religions.  It is notably absent from Rimworld Ideology, except for the water primacy meme of Alpha Memes.
